My Day at the Gym

September 23, 2025 | By Alex Student

Today I woke up and stretched, after that I made breakfast which consisted of 2 eggs and a piece of toast. While I was eating my roommate came out of his room and greeted me. After that I went to the gym for a workout, then came back home for a shower. Doing some homework after working out tired me out so I ended up doomscrolling on TikTok for a while. Later in the afternoon, I decided to go for a walk in the park to clear my mind. The weather was perfect, and I enjoyed watching people play frisbee and walk their dogs. After returning home, I cooked a simple dinner and spent the evening reading a book. It was a productive and relaxing day overall.

Why I Love Coding

September 22, 2025 | By Alex Student

Coding has always been a passion of mine. I started learning programming in high school, and since then, I've enjoyed solving problems and building projects. The feeling of creating something from scratch and seeing it work is incredibly rewarding. Every day, I discover new technologies and tools that make development more exciting. Whether it's building a website, automating tasks, or analyzing data, coding gives me the freedom to explore and innovate. I also appreciate the collaborative nature of programming. Working with others on open-source projects or in a team setting helps me grow and learn from different perspectives. The tech community is supportive and always willing to help. In the future, I hope to use my coding skills to make a positive impact, whether it's through developing useful applications or teaching others how to code.
